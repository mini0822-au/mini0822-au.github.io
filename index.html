Investment Thesis

Built on the conviction that
geography is an edge

Australia's unique position — proximity to Asia, alignment with the West, and a culture that rewards directness — creates distinct advantages for founders building global companies from the Southern Hemisphere.

We invest at the intersection of local insight and global ambition, bringing strategic advisory, capital, and a network that spans Sydney to Seoul to Silicon Valley.

Strategic Capital
More than a check — we co-build go-to-market strategy, open doors, and stay engaged through critical inflection points.
Cross-Border Bridge
Deep networks across AU, KR, and US markets. We help founders move capital, talent, and customers across borders efficiently.
Sector Focus
Fintech, AI infrastructure, healthtech, and digital commerce — sectors where Australian founders have a measurable edge.
Founder-First
We've been operators. We're allergic to interference and allergic to passivity. We show up when it matters.
